MVP-Tac

A Miniaturized Dual-Modal Vision and Photoelastic Tactile Sensor for
Robot-Assisted Minimally Invasive Surgery

Authors • Purdue University • IROS 2026 (Accepted)

Teaser image/video coming soon.

Abstract

Robot-assisted minimally invasive surgery (RMIS) offers major benefits over open and laparoscopic procedures, yet still lacks tactile feedback. MVP-Tac introduces a compact dual-modal vision and photoelastic tactile sensor that enables co-located vision and tactile sensing for minimally invasive surgery.
